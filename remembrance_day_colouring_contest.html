From its origins as a simple bugle call, the Last Post has developed into one of the most enduring, universal symbols of remembrance. What is the Last Post? The Last Post is a short musical call traditionally played on a bugle, but can also be played on a trumpet. For Remembrance Day ceremonies, the use of “Last Post” and “Reveille” (Rouse) is used to draw the symbolic association between the soldier’s last duty of “sitting sentry” (death) and his “rising” above his mortal duties (reveille).
